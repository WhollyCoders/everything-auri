Everything Auri

I don't know where to start... I know what I like to do but... I like to draw, sew, paint, and bake... I like to sketch but sometimes it doesn't have to be something in particular, it is just something that I make up in my head. I am a person who likes to eat. I think I like to eat because food is delicious. I think I was made to be creative. Not only do I have a desire to exercise my creativity, I also feel compelled to do things that stretch my creative side. I enjoy sewing and had the privledge of creating several pieces with the help of different classes and mentors, but I am hoping to create my own original clothing without assistance in the future. I have created different accessories in the past without patterns, and I am anticipating doing the same with clothing.
